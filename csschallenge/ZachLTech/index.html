Illuminate

A virtual hackathon designed for high-schoolers to showcase their coding skills and creativity

Join Now More Info

Illuminate is a hackathon tailored for high school students to showcase their coding skills and creative ideas. Hosted by Code the Stars, get ready for a weekend packed with engaging coding tasks, informative workshops and great opportunities to network. Come be a part of an event where you can express your imagination and interact with students, from various parts of the world.

Coding Challenges

Throughout this hackathon, you're invited to participate in a few coding challenges which are to be announced throughout the event.

Speaker Workshops

Join us during our special workshops where guest speakers will guide participants through fun and educational activities

Networking Opportunities

Anyone who joins will be able to network themselves via our Discord server and meet new people throughout our event

Schedule
